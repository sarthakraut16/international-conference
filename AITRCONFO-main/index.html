About

In the 21st century, innovation and entrepreneurship are vital for economic growth, social progress, and environmental sustainability. NEP 2020 emphasizes nurturing creativity and critical thinking to foster innovation in learners, making education a foundation for knowledge creation and contributing to the national economy. By prioritizing these aspects, NEP 2020 aims to develop a generation of thinkers and problem-solvers who can drive the nation forward. Traditional educational approaches are inadequate in a rapidly changing world with technological advancements, dynamic global markets, and evolving societal challenges. As the pace of change accelerates, it becomes increasingly clear that merely imparting knowledge is not enough; educational institutions must also focus on developing critical life skills.

Entrepreneurship is essential for economic growth and job creation, as it encourages individuals to take initiative, innovate, and create new opportunities. Research reports highlight the need to revamp education to equip students with 21st-century skills, preparing them for the evolving professional challenges. These skills include adaptability, digital literacy, and the ability to collaborate across diverse teams.

In this context, Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research is organizing an International Conference on ‘Innovation, Incubation, and IPR (ICI³2024: Promoting Sustainability and Self-Reliance). This conference aims to catalyze entrepreneurial transformation in education. By bringing together thought leaders, practitioners, and aspiring entrepreneurs, the event seeks to create a vibrant ecosystem that supports innovation and sustainable development.

The conference offers academics, entrepreneurs, incubators, corporate professionals, faculty, school teachers, students (HEI/School), and intrapreneurs a platform to learn about best practices across the nation, share knowledge, and promote an entrepreneurial mindset. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in interactive sessions, workshops, and networking events designed to foster collaboration and inspire new ideas. It will inspire students to explore their entrepreneurial ambitions at the nascent stage, encouraging them to think beyond conventional career paths and consider the impact they can have through entrepreneurial ventures.

    Paper Submission Guidelines

    1. Abstract should be written in 150-250 words, 12pt. font size and Times New Roman Font with 1.5 line spacing with 4 to 5 keywords.
    2. The research papers must be written within the word limit of 2000-3000 words, 12pt. font size and Times New Roman Font with 1.15 line spacing.
    3. The first page of the paper must incorporate the title of the paper, name of the authors, affiliation and email address.
    4. Tables, illustrations, charts and figures should be serially numbered and duly acknowledged.
    5. Only original and unpublished papers will be considered. It should be plagiarism free and not AI generated.
    6. Authors will present their papers at the conference. The selected papers will be published in conference proceedings having ISBN number.
